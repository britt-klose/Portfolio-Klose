About Me


I recently graduated from college at the University of Tampa, in May of 2021 with a degreee in marketing. After moving back home to Pennsylvania and taking some time off, I realized I wanted to explore more areas in the business field, specifically something more into technology. Coding was something I had always wanted to try, but had never done before. I decided now was the perfect time to go for it, and began the Coding Bootcamp with Upenn. Though it is a big change from marketing I quickly found it fascinating to be on the other end of the user experience. The skills I have learned so far can be seen firsthand in the projects under Work Experience. Feel free to scroll down and follow the link to my GitHub pages if you want to see more!
